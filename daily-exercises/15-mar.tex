$L = \{<p,c> | c\ \text{is the caste of the person}\ p\}$. Show that membership in L is undecidable.

\textbf{Answer:}

Consider the person $p$ whose caste is $c \iff$ $p$'s caste is not $c$. Membership for any caste $c$ for this person is undecidable, and thus this language is undecidable.
